Full Screen Mode

Normally, at startup BOOM runs in full screen mode. Depending on your screen resolution, the actual game area, which is sized 640x480, enlarges as much as possible for the best gaming experience.

You can switch from full screen to windowed mode (and back) at any time, even during a game, by selecting the command "Toggle Full Screen" from the Windows menu (Command-F).

When in full screen mode the menubar is normally hidden and can be revealed by moving the cursor to the top of the screen.
