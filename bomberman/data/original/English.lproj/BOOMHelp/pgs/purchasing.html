Purchasing BOOM

The shareware fee for BOOM is US$9.95 for a single user license. Site and World-Wide licenses are also available.

BOOM can be purchased from my reseller - Kagi.

Purchasing BOOM from Kagi is easy, fast and secure.
Kagi accepts all major credit cards, checks and cash in a variety of currencies.
Please review the various purchasing options below before placing your order.
Thank you in advance for your support!

If you already purchased the game and need information on how to use your registration code to unlock it, see "Registering BOOM".

Instant purchasing

If you decide to purchase a single user licence with your credit card, the easiest way to place your order is via the built-in order form. Using this option, you are allowed to purchase and register the game instantly, without ever leaving the application.
Instant purchasing is only available for single orders placed with a credit card. Orders for multiple copies or with an alternative payment method must be submitted online.

To buy BOOM, start the game application and click on the "Buy BOOM..." button in the Shareware Reminder dialog. As an alternative, you can select the corresponding command from the Application menu.
Make sure you have an active Internet connection, otherwise you will not be able to place your order.

During the purchasing process, the application interface may be localized for your native language, drawn from your system configuration. Currently supported languages are: English (US and international), Japanese, German, French and Italian. The following instructions are relative to the US English localization.

You are first presented with a dialog that gives you the opportunity to buy the game online (see next section). If you decide to continue and purchase the game instantly, click on the "Buy Now" button.

When the built-in order form appears, please enter all the required information and click on the "Purchase" button to confirm your order.
If the order is processed without errors and your credit card is successfully charged, the game is unlocked instantly! There is no need to manually register the game!

To keep your credit card information safe and protect you from fraud, orders are submitted to Kagi through an SSL (Secure Socket Layer) connection. This means that all communication to and from the remote server is encrypted so that nobody can steal your credit card information during the transaction.

Purchasing online

You can easily and safely buy BOOM online at my Kagi Online Order Page.

To buy BOOM online using your Web browser you can click on the above link or start the game application and click on the "Buy BOOM..." button in the Shareware Reminder dialog. As an alternative, you can select the corresponding command from the Application menu.
Make sure you have an active Internet connection, otherwise you will not be able to place your order.

You are first presented with a dialog that gives you the opportunity to buy the game instantly (see previous section). To proceed to my online order page at Kagi, click on the "Buy Online" button.

If you prefer, you can visit my web site (see "Contact info") and order the game directly from there.
