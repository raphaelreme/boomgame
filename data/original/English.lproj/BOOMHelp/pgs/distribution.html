Distribution

You can distribute BOOM freely, provided that you leave the software and the accompanying documentation unchanged. You can include BOOM in CD-ROMs and other software collections only by previous agreement.

BOOM is SHAREWARE, which means that you are allowed to use it on a trial basis for up to 30 days. After this period, if you want to continue using it, you are expected to pay the required shareware fee (see "Purchasing BOOM").
